About

Behind the Kit

From the very early ages, drums and rhythm have inspired me — and made me play drums too. I started at 5 years old. I was too young for music school at the time, so a friend of my parents showed me the basics until I could take official lessons.

From there, I studied for years under a number of drummers, sharpening technique, feel, and versatility across styles.

Trained Under

Pit Furrer & Rafael Woll — both well known in the Lucerne drumming scene and beyond.
